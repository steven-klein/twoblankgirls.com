/October 26, 2017/

018: Food Court

/Season 1/

In the eighteenth episode of Two Bulk Girls it’s FOOD COURT.  The Bulk Girls go hard on almost every item on the menu. And there’s an extra special guest in the form of Annitsa’s mom! Listen as they talk electric car mishaps, public rule enforcement and Salsa and Beer.

Two Bulk Girls is the podcast about two best friends attempting to conquer the Costco lifestyle, one giant item and one week at a time.

015: Brit Goes Bulk

/Season 1/

In the fifteenth episode of Two Bulk Girls, Jenna and Annitsa try something a little different. Instead of a week with their items they have a half hour with their friend Colin—-a Scottish/British gent living in NYC. He doesn’t understand Costco or the podcast, so the Bulk Girls make him try Vienna Sausages from a can and Fruit by the Foot.  Listen as he hates it all and makes the girls look like the horrible Americans that they most likely are.

About Us

We are Jenna and Annitsa, two best friends fascinated by how we feel at any moment. Being a person is hard--it's up and down and sideways and diagonal. We want to talk about it! And our hope is that you can relate too. Each week we'll fill in the blank, and anything related to that word is what we'll cover. You'll learn about us, about what's happening in the world and probably TMI, as the kids say.
